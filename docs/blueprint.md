# **App Name**: DAGM Recruitment Portal

## Core Features:

- User Data Input: Collect and store personal data from applicants, including name, contact information, and background details.
- Department Selection: Allow applicants to select their preferred department within DAGM.
- Essay Submission: Enable applicants to submit essays outlining their reasons for choosing a department, proposed work programs, and motivations.
- Case Study Response: Present applicants with critical thinking questions related to public policy and time management.
- Commitment Confirmation: Require applicants to confirm their commitment to DAGM.
- Form Validation: Use client-side validation to ensure all required fields are filled correctly before submission.
- Personalized Feedback Generation: Provide an AI-driven tool to analyze the candidate's essays and generate feedback based on criteria such as clarity, relevance, and originality, providing a personalized assessment to both the recruitment team and, optionally, the candidate. It will generate an structured representation of feedback (e.g. using JSON).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5) to convey trust and professionalism, reflecting the organization's mission to represent the youth.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F5F5F5), providing a clean and modern look while ensuring readability and focus on the content.
- Accent color: Electric Blue (#7DF9FF), used sparingly for interactive elements and highlights to draw attention without overwhelming the user.
- Body and headline font: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern, clean, and readable text.
- Use minimalist, consistent icons throughout the interface to represent different departments and actions.
- Implement a clear, step-by-step layout to guide users through the recruitment process, with a visible progress indicator.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and provide feedback during form interactions.
